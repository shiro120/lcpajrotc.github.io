                                                         Leadership Education & Training

(Leadership, duty and responsibility don’t have an expiration date and neither should we!)
 * 14 Leadership Traits:
Bearing                  Courage               Decisiveness            Dependability            Endurance      Enthusiasm  Initiative               Integrity               Judgment                        Justice                    Knowledge          Loyalty   
                                                                  Tact                         Unselfishness 


 *Seven Army Values: Loyalty   Duty   Respect   Selfless Service   Honor   Integrity   Personal Courage
 
 First Aid:   *Seven Life-Saving steps:
  1. Check for see if the Victim is Consciousness
  2. Check for Breathing, Heartbeat and Pulse
  3. Check for Bleeding
  4. Check for the following Signs of Shock
  5. Check for Fractures (Broken Bones)
  6. Check for Burns.  If You Find Burns, Cover Them with a Clean Dry Cloth
  7. Check for Head Injuries

​
  *11 Leadership Principles (Accomplishment of the Mission  and the Health and Welfare of the Team)


  1. Know Yourself and Seek Self-Improvement
  2. Be Technically and Tactfully Proficient
  3. Know Your Subordinates and Look Out For Their welfare
  4. Keep Your Subordinates Informed
  5. Set the Example
  6. Ensure the Task is Understood, Supervised and Accomplished
  7. Train Your Subordinates as a Team
  8. Make Sound and Timely Decisions
  9. Seek Responsibility and Take Responsibility for Your Actions
  10. Develop A Sense of Responsibility In Your Subordinates
  11. Employ Your Commands in Accordance with its Capabilities
